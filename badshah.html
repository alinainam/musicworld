Description

Whenever we talk about some of the top Indian rappers in the industry, the first name that pops up in our mind is Badshah. With his extraordinary rapping skills, he surely has created a great “buzz” in the Indian music industry. His songs have made the audience go “Paagal” on the dance floor, showering him with immense love and praise. Badshah has sung hit Punjabi, Hindi, and Haryanvi songs which has made everyone get on the dance floor and shake a leg along them. Badshah’s real name is Aditya Prateek Singh Sisodia and he hails from village Kalsi in Karnal, Haryana, India. He completed his schooling from Bal Bharti Public School, Pitampura, New Delhi. During his school days, he used to perform in his school choir and thought that he could also pursue music as his career. Badshah earlier enrolled in the Banaras Hindu University as a math student but later pursued civil engineering from Punjab Engineering College. It was at that time he got exposed to new Punjabi music which encouraged him to take on rap writing. Badshah started his career in the year 2006 alongside renowned singer Yo Yo Honey Singh in his hip hop group Mafia Mundeer. Later in 2012, he split from Honey Singh and released his independent Haryanvi song Kar Gayi Chull, which was later used in the movie Kapoor & Sons. Badshah has made some marvelous contributions in the Indian music industry with hit songs, popularly known as hit Badshah songs. His debut single, DJ Wale Babu, in collaboration with Aastha Gill topped the Indian iTunes chart within 24 hours of its release. He then collaborated with Navv Inder for the song “Wakhra Swag” which received many accolades. Slowly and gradually, Badshah rose to fame and entered Bollywood giving some marvelous contributions which were simply irresistible. There are a lot of songs which aren’t famous for their name but by their artists’ name. Same is in the case of Badshah. His songs are popularly searched as hit Badshah songs or new Badshah songs. There are many latest Badshah songs which have made a special place not just in the hearts of people but in their playlists as well. Some of the most popular Badshah songs are Garmi, Chandigarh Mein, Kamaal, Akh Lad Jaave, Tareefan, Kar Gayi Chull, Heartless, Kala Chashma, Sheher Ki Ladki, Bad Boy, and the list goes on.
